# Market Entry Strategy for Zepto in Telangana

## Objective

Evaluate whether Zepto should expand aggressively in Telangana.

## Scope

-   Market sizing
-   Customer analysis
-   Competitor benchmarking
-   SWOT analysis
-   Strategic recommendations

## Key Findings

-   Hyderabad offers the largest addressable market.
-   Warangal provides a promising secondary expansion opportunity.
-   Students and young professionals are the primary target segments.
-   Competition exists but demand continues to grow.

## Recommendation

Launch in Hyderabad first, followed by phased expansion into major
Tier-2 cities.
